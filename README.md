# Task 3 - Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Analysis
## Future Interns | Data Science & Analytics Track

## Overview
Analyzed a full marketing funnel across 5 stages and 5 channels
to identify conversion drop-offs and opportunities for growth.

## Tools Used
- Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, NumPy)
- Google Colab

## Key Insights
- Overall funnel conversion rate: 2.2%
- Biggest drop-off: Visitors to Leads (58% drop)
- Email marketing had the highest conversion rate
- Social Media generated the most leads
- Conversion rate grew consistently from Jan to Jun

## Recommendations
1. Improve landing pages to convert more visitors to leads
2. Invest more budget in Email marketing
3. Better nurture Prospects to increase qualification rate
4. Replicate June strategies to sustain upward trend
